About Brayan

Born in El Salvador, I immigrated to the United States at the age of six, carrying with me a curiosity that’s shaped my life. As a child, I was fascinated by how things worked, often taking apart my PlayStation 2 (much to my parents’ dismay) or building makeshift radios from spare parts scavenged from bins. This love for engineering and problem-solving has stayed with me, driving me to work hard and approach every challenge with my best effort. I take pride in doing things right, and I’m not one to sit idle—I thrive on staying busy and pushing myself to grow.

Today, I’m driven by big dreams and a desire to make a meaningful impact. Whether through innovative technology or simply lending a hand, I’m committed to leaving the world a better place than I found it.

— Brayan Villatoro
